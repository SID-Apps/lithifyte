01 — Approach

This isn't a spreadsheet. It's a living map.

It starts with one node — your household. Everything else grows out of it as you scroll. Grab any node and pull it; let go and it settles back.

02 — Household

People — then their accounts.

Each person forms as a node, and their current, savings, credit and cash accounts branch off — carrying real balances. Hover any node to read it.

03 — Categories & transactions

Category hubs bloom into shops.

Spending flows out to category hubs — Groceries, Home, Transport — then to the merchants behind them. Watch the transactions pulse down the links.

04 — Goals & debts

Goals wear their progress. Debts are holes.

A goal carries its funding as a gold ring that fills as you save. Debts are drawn hollow — an honest hole in your net worth until they're paid off.

05 — Flow

Income splits, to the cent.

Keep scrolling to watch €6,000 of income branch out into every category and into savings. Overspend doesn't vanish — it's drawn from balance, labelled honestly.

08 — What's inside

One page. A full finance engine.

Living money map

Household → people → accounts → categories → merchants. Search it, drag it, watch transactions pulse along the links.

Budgets that pace themselves

Suggestions from your real medians, safe-to-spend per remaining day, and alerts that know a fixed bill from an overspend.

Cautious forecasts

Recurring bills detected automatically, seasonal uplift, and a buffer sized from your irregular spending — not wishful averages.

Debt payoff planner

Avalanche vs snowball with real amortisation, payoff dates, and an honest flag when a payment doesn't even cover interest.

Net worth & holdings

Layered net worth, goals with progress, holdings with gain and tax estimates — stale data flagged, never guessed away.

Import & export anything

Bank CSV statements in — with dedupe on re-upload and transfer matching. Excel and CSV packs out, plus encrypted backups.

Optional local AI

Categorise with a model you run yourself (Ollama, LM Studio). Assist-only, never required, and never remote by default.

Trust you can read

102 self-tests run in your own browser. Open source under AGPL-3.0. No analytics, no tracking, no server for your data.
