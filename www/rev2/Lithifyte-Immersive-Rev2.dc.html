01 — Approach

This isn't a spreadsheet. It's a living map.

It starts with one node — your household. Everything else grows out of it as you scroll. Grab any node and pull it; let go and it settles back.

02 — Household

People — then their accounts.

Each person forms as a node, and their current, savings, credit and cash accounts branch off — carrying real balances. Hover any node to read it.

03 — Categories & transactions

Category hubs bloom into shops.

Spending flows out to category hubs — Groceries, Home, Transport — then to the merchants behind them. Watch the transactions pulse down the links.

04 — Goals & debts

Goals wear their progress. Debts are holes.

A goal carries its funding as a gold ring that fills as you save. Debts are drawn hollow — an honest hole in your net worth until they're paid off.

05 — Flow

Income splits, to the cent.

Keep scrolling to watch €6,000 of income branch out into every category and into savings. Overspend doesn't vanish — it's drawn from balance, labelled honestly.

06 — The promise

Your bank data never
leaves the browser.

Statements stay on your device, optionally encrypted at rest. There is no finance backend to send them to — that's not a toggle. Read the source and verify every word.

07 — Docking complete

Enter the platform.

Start with the demo household, or create free access. We store only your email for sign-in — nothing else.
