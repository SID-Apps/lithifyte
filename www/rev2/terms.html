The service

Your data, your device

Lithifyte never receives, stores, or processes your financial data on any server. Everything you import stays in your browser's storage on your device. This is a design guarantee, and it has a flip side you accept by using the app:

  • You are responsible for backups. The app can export (optionally encrypted) backup files. If your browser storage is cleared and you have no backup, your data is gone — we cannot restore it, because we never had it.
  • If you enable the encrypted at-rest lock and forget the passphrase, the data cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.

Not financial advice

Lithifyte computes budgets, forecasts, debt schedules, and tax estimates from the data and assumptions you give it. These are informational tools, not financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Figures can be wrong — through bugs, through your data, or through simplified models (tax rules in particular are simplified and may not match your situation). Verify anything important with a qualified professional before acting on it.
