07 · Threat model

Your bank data never leaves the browser.

Statements stay in local storage on your device. Optional passphrase encryption locks them at rest. Backups are files you export. The page does not phone home with transactions — that is not a toggle; there is no finance backend to send them to.

Free account = email onlyWe may keep your email so you can sign in and we can reach you. Never your statements, balances, or merchants.
Finance stays localThe product still computes on-device. Cloud identity is a door key — not a vault for your money map.
